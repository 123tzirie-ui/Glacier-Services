About Glacier Services

Dedicated to professional property management and community success

Our Mission

Glacier Services exists to deliver professional, transparent, and responsive property management that allows property owners and boards to focus on what matters most: their communities and investments.

We believe that great property management combines local expertise, modern technology, and genuine care for the communities we serve. Since our founding in 2010, we've built lasting relationships based on trust, reliability, and exceptional service.

Our commitment is simple: manage your property like we own it, communicate with transparency, and always deliver on our promises.

Our Story

Founded in 2010 by property management professionals with a vision for better service, Glacier Services has grown to manage over 300 properties and serve thousands of residents. What started as a local operation has expanded while maintaining the personalized, attentive service that built our reputation. Today, we combine decades of collective experience with cutting-edge technology to deliver the best property management in the region.
