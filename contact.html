Common Questions

What's the best way to reach you if there's an emergency?

For maintenance emergencies after hours, call our emergency hotline at (555) 123-4567 Option 1. For other urgent issues, try our main number first, then email support@glacierservices.com.

Can we schedule an in-person meeting?

Absolutely! We can typically accommodate meetings within 48 hours of your request. Call us or use the contact form to schedule an appointment at our office or your community.

Do you offer phone consultations?

Yes! Many clients prefer phone or video consultations. Let us know your preference when you contact us and we'll arrange the most convenient option.

How long does it usually take to hear back from you?

We aim to respond to all inquiries within 24 hours during business days. Urgent matters are typically handled within hours. For faster response, calling our office directly is best.
