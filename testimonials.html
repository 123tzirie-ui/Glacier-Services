Case Studies

How we helped communities achieve their goals

Improving Financial Health at Meadowbrook Community

The Challenge: Meadowbrook's HOA was struggling with unclear financial reporting, inadequate reserve planning, and member frustration about assessments. The board had no clear visibility into where money was being spent or whether they were financially sustainable long-term.

Our Solution: Glacier Services implemented comprehensive financial management including monthly reporting, budget analysis, and a formal reserve study. We identified cost savings through vendor renegotiation and presented a transparent path to financial stability.

The Result: Within 18 months, Meadowbrook had a fully funded reserve, member satisfaction increased 40%, and the board implemented assessments with community confidence. Members now have full visibility into financial decisions.

Streamlining Operations at Riverside Condominiums

The Challenge: Riverside Condominiums switched from self-management to Glacier Services because they were spending 30+ hours per month on management duties. They had no property management software and were missing important deadlines and maintenance.

Our Solution: We implemented our modern management portal, established vendor relationships, created maintenance schedules, and provided board support. We handled all day-to-day operations, freeing the board to focus on strategic decisions.

The Result: The board now spends just 4-5 hours monthly on management. Maintenance response time dropped from 2 weeks to 2 days. Member satisfaction surveys improved 50%, and management costs were 20% less than self-management.

Transitioning to Professional Management at Westwood Townhomes

The Challenge: Westwood needed to transition from their previous management company due to poor communication and hidden fees. They were concerned about disruption and wanted a smooth, transparent transition.

Our Solution: Glacier Services managed the entire transition process, coordinating with the previous manager, reviewing all financial records, and implementing new systems. We provided transparency reports at every step and maintained continuity of service.

The Result: The transition was completed in just 3 weeks with zero service disruption. Westwood discovered hidden fees from the previous manager totaling $15,000 annually, which they recovered. After 5 years with us, they remain a valued client.
